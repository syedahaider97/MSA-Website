Wahed Invest

Wahed Invest

The battle of Wahed Invest occurred on the 12th of Jumada Al-Thani, 1439 After Hijra, as a type of civil war between the MSAs of America, each competing to get the most Facebook tags and win $5,000. Through hard work, brotherhood, and sisterhood, The NJIT MSA came out victorious, defeating every other MSA in the country.

NJIT Student Organization of the Year

Student Orginization
Of The Year

This award is given to one student organization which exemplifies commitment to the betterment and strengthening of the organization. The organization serves as a role model to other NJIT student organizations by excelling in group leadership, fulfilling the mission and goals of the organization, and engaging in teamwork and collaboration with other student organizations. Alhamdulliah with the help of our members MSA won the NJIT Organization of the Year award for the academic year 2017-2018.
